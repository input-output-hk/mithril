Handle discrepancies at startup in the epoch settings store.

In case an aggregator has been launched after some epochs of not being up or at initial startup, the discrepancies in the epoch settings store needs to be fixed.

The epoch settings needs to be recorded for the working epoch and the next 3 epochs. We need data over four epochs because the epoch service use epoch settings over a window of three epochs, and there may be an epoch change between this handle_discrepancies_at_startup call and the epoch service call.
